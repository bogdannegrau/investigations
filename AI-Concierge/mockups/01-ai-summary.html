✦ AI Overview

There are several reasons your bet may not have paid out as expected:

1. Dead Heat

  • What it means: Two or more selections tied for a position, so winnings are divided proportionally.

2. Rule 4 Deduction

  • What it means: A runner was withdrawn late from a horse race, and a deduction is applied to your winnings.

3. Non-Runner / Void Selection

  • What it means: If a selection didn't participate, it's voided. In accumulators this reduces your bet to the remaining legs.
