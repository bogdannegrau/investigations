what happened to my bets
✦ AI Overview

There are several reasons your bet may not have paid out as expected. Here's a checklist of the most common causes:

1. Dead Heat

  • What it means: Two or more selections tied for a position, so your winnings are divided proportionally between the joint winners.

2. Rule 4 Deduction

  • What it means: A runner was withdrawn late from a horse race, and a deduction is applied to your winnings. The amount depends on the withdrawn horse's price.

3. Non-Runner / Void Selection

  • What it means: If a selection didn't participate, it's voided. In accumulators this reduces your bet to the remaining selections.
